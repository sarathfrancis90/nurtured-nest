Meet Your Guide

Varshitha —
The Heart of Nest

Nurtured Nest was born from a belief that every birth experience deserves an attentive, knowledgeable guide. Varshitha founded this practice to create the sanctuary she wished existed — a space where modern parents feel fully supported, seen, and safe.

With training in evidence-based doula care and a background in maternal wellness, Varshitha brings warmth, clinical knowledge, and deep respect for each family's unique path. Based in Toronto, she serves families across the Greater Toronto Area.
