Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Introduction

Nurtured Nest ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website or use our doula services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, and phone number when you reach out to us via phone, email, or booking a consultation.
  • Health-related information — details you share with us during consultations related to your pregnancy, birth preferences, and postpartum needs. This information is shared voluntarily and kept strictly confidential.
  • Website analytics — we use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics tool. It collects no personal data, uses no cookies, and is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PIPEDA. No consent banner is needed.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations
  • To provide personalized doula care and birth support services
  • To communicate with you about your care plan
  • To improve our website and services based on anonymous usage data

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies. Our analytics provider (Plausible) is entirely cookie-free and does not track individual visitors.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or with your explicit consent. Health-related information shared during our professional relationship is treated with the same confidentiality as any healthcare provider relationship.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for the use of your information
